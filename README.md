# Project Overview  

A curated collection of Sales, Revenue & Retail Analytics Projects demonstrating skills in data cleaning, modeling, visualization, and business insight generation.

Pipelines lie. Not on purpose, but a number that looks healthy on a dashboard can hide deals that are quietly dying and reps who are busy without being productive. The projects in this section exist to close that gap, whether the context is a B2B sales pipeline or a multi-location retail chain..  

---

## 💼 About This Portfolio  

This portfolio reflects my ability to: 
- Separate what looks like performance from what actually is
- Build reports that surface risk early, not in the post-mortem
- Connect revenue numbers back to the specific decision they should drive
